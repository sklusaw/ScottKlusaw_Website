Who Is This Handsome Devil?

I specialize in building and maintaining software applications, having worked with companies such as Union Pacific Railroad and Orion Advisor Services. When I have time, I also do pro-bono work for various charities such as Habitat for Humanity, the Open Door Mission, and various Nebraskan music groups.


Drumming has been one of my deep passions since 2008, specializing in the styles of rock, classic rock, religious, and metal. Check out my Youtube for some of my music! In mid 2014, I began to write fiction in various places online with various pen names. Since then, I've accumulated over one million reads of my work and have been published in the Los Altos Town Crier under my name.
